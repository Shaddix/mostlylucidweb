Just rediscovered this album after many years away from it, my opinion this is one of the greatest albums of all time (not something I say flippantly). [The Longpigs  - The Sun Is Often Out](http://www.amazon.co.uk/exec/obidos/ASIN/B000001EJB/mostlylucid-21) ([us link](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B000001EJB/mostlylucid-21)), absolutely amazing, doesn't sound a single bit dated. "Lost Myself" and "On and On" are just heartbreakingly good with the latter being just one of the best love songs ever...ah well...jjust a suggestion :-)
