## Contract Pain

I won't go into WHY that contract fell through but it was a new one on me in my 15 years of freelancing; they didn't like a tweet! I don't like the idea of references; to me they seem outdated and especially with a career as long and storied as mine, I don't think they're necessary.

Probably a good thing in the long run as I don't think I would have enjoyed working with them.
