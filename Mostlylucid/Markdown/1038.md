Really useful service, chances are when you design a website you don't have access to the dozens of browser / platform combinations that your users do. [Browsercam](http://www.browsercam.com/)(though commercial) offers the only truly viable way I've ever seen to do this, put simply you enter a URL and this service displays your site (or just a single page) in Six different browsers, three different operating systems, with and without flash. Very nice!
