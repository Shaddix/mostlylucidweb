Well, I'm suffering from post new year sleeping pattern - I basically become nocturnal over the Christmas and New Year (Hogmanay) holidays, have to start work in 8 hours...not good! Anyway, while I am sitting here awake (there's a show on about Turkish Harems; not as tittilating as it sounds, very political), thought I'd embark on a hunt for an new RSS aggregator.

I've been using [Feeddemon](http://www.bradsoft.com/feeddemon/index.asp) during the Beta period, it's very nice, pretty interface etc...however it doesn't support CommentAPI and I'm not sure I want to pay $29.95 when there's comparable free products.
I've now installed [RSSBandit](http://www.rssbandit.org/), it's pretty nice but nowhere near as polished as Feeddemon - why can't I expand the Treeview??? It's in the lead so far...
[SharpReader](http://www.sharpreader.net/), hmm...still usies way too much memory, but it does have a nice [w.bloggar plugin](http://www.sharpreader.net/wBloggarPlugin.zip)and support for more bloggy type stuff!

I guess it's still early days for this type of product (I can't consider stuff like [NewsGator](http://www.newsgator.com/) right now due to some mail config stuff (I want to keep stuff away from my Exchange server)...anyone else using interesting aggregators?
