## 
Calling all Scottish .NET developers 

posted on Saturday, September 25, 2004 7:17 PM

Reading Paschal's stuff on his [Irish Developers group](http://weblogs.asp.net/pleloup/archive/2004/09/25/234183.aspx),  this seems like a great idea. So, here's mine - I know there's lots of [Scottish Developers](http://www.scottishdevelopers.com/) and of those, a lot are .NET developers; what I don't know though is where their blogs are so what I want to do is put togther a list of all of these blogs; which I'll put in the right hand column and link from all of my future posts -as well as provide a page for seeing aggregated content from all those blogs. Only qualification is that you have to be Scottish (i.e., you have to have been born in Scotland) and be a developer working in .NET. If you're interested please email me at scottishdotnet@mostlylucid.co.uk - oh, also email me if you don't yet have a blog but would like one (if I get enough requests I'd be happy to set up an aggregated ala [Weblogs.asp.net](http://weblogs.asp.net/) blog for these users).
