Not content with producing some of the [best free ASP.NET tools around](http://www.excentricsworld.com/), [Matt's](http://weblogs.asp.net/mhawley/) only gone and made a [fantastic little utility](http://weblogs.asp.net/mhawley/archive/2004/03/08/85986.aspx) to make deploying web sites much easier.
