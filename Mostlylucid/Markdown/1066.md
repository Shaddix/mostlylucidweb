Was perusing the ASP.NET Forums earlier when I stumbled across [this post](http://www.asp.net/Forums/ShowPost.aspx?tabindex=1&amp;PostID=592296) by Revjon (of [FreeTextBox](http://www.freetextbox.com) fame), his question was on whether plans exist for integrating FTB 2.0 into .TEXT. Well, I went ahead and did this for 0.95 - not perfect (there's a Javascript popup which I'll have to figure out how to get rid of) but gets the job done (and is cross-browser compatible). If you want to have a play, the files you need are in [this archive](/uploads/TEXT095FTB20.zip) - should (fingers crossed, I haven't tested this much!) just be a copy into your .TEXT directory (back up your original just in case though!).
